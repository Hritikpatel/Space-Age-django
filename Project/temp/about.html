Our Vision

For the Benefit of Earth

Space Age was founded with a vision of millions of people living and working in space for the benefit of Earth. Space Age envisions a time when people can tap into the limitless resources of space and enable the movement of damaging industries into space to preserve Earth, humanity’s Space Age.

Our Mission

Increase Access to Space

Space Age is working today to create that future where all civil, commercial, and defense customers can travel to space. Our efforts include flying everyone to space, developing an orbital launch vehicle, building next-generation space habitats, and finding new habitats.

Our Foundation

Club for the Future

Founded by Space Age in 2019, Club for the Future is a foundation whose mission is to inspire future generations to pursue careers in STEM and to help invent the future of life in space. The Club and its collaborators are doing this through Postcards to Space, space-focused curriculum, and access to space on Space Age’s rockets.
